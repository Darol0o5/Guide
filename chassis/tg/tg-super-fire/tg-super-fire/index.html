    TG Super FIRE¶

    Quick facts¶

    • Developed by: TG Racing
    • Release: September 2024
    • Origin: China
    • Status: Available
    • Production: Batch
    • Scale: 1/24-1/28
    • Body mounting: Magnet mounting, L-brackets, Kyosho mounts(optional)
    • Materials: - Aluminum & carbon fibre

    Adjustability¶

    At-a-glance¶

    • Wheelbase: ✅
    • Camber: Front ✅ / Rear ✅
    • Toe: Front ✅ / Rear ✅
    • Caster: ✅
    • Ackermann quick adjustment: ✅
    • Ride height: Front ✅ / Rear ✅
    • Track width: Front ✅ / Rear ✅
    • Front shocks: preload ✅
    • Rear shocks: preload ✅ / angle ✅
    • Active systems: ❌
    • Motor position: mid ✅ / high ✅ / rear ✅
    • Servo position: ❌
    • Pinion-Spur distance: ✅
    • Front Knuckle KPI hinge point: ❌
    • Front knuckle steering linkage hinge point: ❌
    • Steering rack linkage hinge point: ❌
    • Extendable dogbones: ✅

    Details¶

    • Wheelbase adjustment method: slider
    • Wheelbase range: 93–108 mm (up to 122mm with wide track upgrade)
    • Track width range: 72–85mm (up to 90mm with wide track upgrade)
    • Caster adjustment: stepless
    • Ackermann adjustment: stepless
    • Rear toe behavior: unknown

    Drivetrain¶

    • Gearbox type: gear-driven(metal gears)
    • Motor orientation: transverse
    • Forces: pro-torque
    • Reversible: ✅
    • Differential: spool

    Steering¶

    • Steering method: pivoted
    • Steering type: bellcrank
    • Servo position: lower deck

    Suspension¶

    • Front: double wishbone, independent, 2 direct-acting shocks
    • Rear: multi-link, independent, 2 direct-acting shocks
    • Shocks type: friction shocks

    Notes¶

    • Two sets of springs supplied
    • A few users complained about the stock shocks not being good enough
    • A wide-track Upgrade available, sold separately
