# K-EP Sleipnir II







![K-EP Sleipnir II](sleipnir-ii.jpg){ width="500" }







## Quick facts







- **Developed by:** *K-Enhance Project(Al Kautsar Kilimajaro)*



- **Release:** *September 2021*



- **Origin:** *Indonesia*



- **Status:** *Discontinued*



- **Production:** *Pre-order*



- **Scale:** *1/24-1/28*



- **Body mounting:** *Magnet mounting/MINI-Z*



- **Materials:** *PLA, carbon version(lower deck, upper deck, motor mount)*







---







## Adjustability







### At-a-glance



- **Wheelbase:** ✅



- **Camber:** Front ✅ / Rear ✅



- **Toe:** Front ✅ / Rear ❌ (fixed 2°)



- **Caster:** ✅



- **Ackermann quick adjustment:** ✅



- **Ride height:** Front ✅ / Rear ✅



- **Track width:** Front ✅ / Rear ✅



- **Front shocks:** preload ✅ / angle ❌



- **Rear shocks:** preload ✅ / angle ✅(2 vertical shocks, monoshock, or 3 shocks setup possible)



- **Active systems:** ❌



- **Motor position:** mid ❌ / high ❌ / rear ✅



- **Servo position:** ❌



- **Pinion-Spur distance:** ✅



- **Front knuckle KPI hinge point:** ❌



- **Front knuckle steering linkage hinge point:** ❌ 



- **Steering rack linkage hinge point:** ❌

## Drivetrain



- **Gearbox type:** *gear-driven(plastic gears)*



- **Motor orientation:** *transverse*



- **Forces:** *anti-torque*



- **Reversible:** ❌



- **Differential:** *open / locked*







---







## Steering



- **Steering method:** *pivoted*



- **Steering system:** *bellcrank*



- **Servo position:** *upper deck*







---







## Suspension



- **Front:** *double wishbone, cantilever(push rods), 2 shocks*



- **Rear:** *double wishbone, independent, 1/2/3 shocks*



- **Shocks type:** *friction shocks*







## Notes





Front end redesign with cantilever suspension, that doesn't even have bulkhead. Just bellcrank and arms!



- **Front suspension update:** *double wishbone, independent, cantilever(pushrod) 2 shocks*



- carbon fiber version came later, equipped with lower deck, upper deck and motor mount.











**ADS Sleipnir III**



Later, K-EP and Bulan Racing fused, creating the ADS - Advanced Development System.



Then the most renown version of Sleipnir was born: [ADS Sleipnir III](sleipnir-iii.md)
